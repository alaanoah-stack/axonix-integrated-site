Project 01 — Warehouse / Industrial

Frank's Warehouse — Private Headquarters Complex

Metro Detroit, MI  ·  2024  ·  Mixed-Use Private Headquarters

Challenge

Large mixed-use private headquarters required comprehensive video coverage across warehouse, vehicle staging, executive offices, and restricted zones — with specific privacy exclusions and access control integration at all entry points.

Solution

Project 03 — Retail

Retail Chain Infrastructure Rollout — 6-Location Buildout

Michigan & Ohio  ·  2023–2024  ·  Retail Chain

Challenge

Growing retail chain needed consistent infrastructure across 6 new locations. Different contractors at each prior site produced inconsistent deployments, creating ongoing IT support overhead and no standardized process for new stores.

Solution

Developed a templated infrastructure design — standardized Cat6A cable plant, identical switch configuration, uniform camera placement by floor plan type, and identical IDF documentation across all 6 locations.

Result

6 locations with identical infrastructure. IT director manages all locations from one platform using the same process. Each site deployed 20% faster than prior multi-contractor deployments.

Project 04 — Corporate Office

Corporate Office Campus — Security & Infrastructure Upgrade

Oakland County, MI  ·  2024  ·  Corporate Office

Challenge

Three-building corporate campus with analog CCTV, an undocumented Cat5e cable plant, no PoE infrastructure for planned IP camera upgrade, and a flat network with no VLAN separation between surveillance, corporate, and guest traffic.

Solution

Full Cat6A recabling of all three buildings with fiber interconnect between MDFs. New managed switching infrastructure with dedicated surveillance VLAN and PoE budget. 32 Axis IP cameras deployed with cloud VMS and 45-day retention.

Result

Complete camera coverage across all three buildings with 4x the resolution of the prior system. Structured cabling now supports 10Gb backbone. Full as-built documentation delivered including fiber OTDR results.
