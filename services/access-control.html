The Problem

Keys Don't Scale. Rekeying Is Expensive. Neither Tells You Who Was Where.

Most commercial facilities still operate on mechanical keys or basic keypad codes. When an employee leaves, you either rekey the building or hope they don't come back. When an incident happens in a restricted area, you have no record of who accessed it or when.

For multi-site operators the problem compounds — different key systems at every location, no unified credential management, and zero visibility into access patterns across your portfolio.

Our Approach

Credential Control at the Door. Visibility Across the Portfolio.

We design access control systems around your operational hierarchy — which roles access which zones, on which schedules, with which credential type. Remote revocation in seconds. Complete access audit log. Scheduled auto-lock on every door. Multi-site visibility from a single dashboard.

Technical Capabilities

Access Control Systems We Design and Deploy

Reader & Credential Types

  • RFID card and key fob (HID, MIFARE, DESFire)
  • Mobile credential (BLE/NFC smartphone unlock)
  • Fingerprint and palm biometric readers
  • PIN + card two-factor configurations
  • Video intercom with remote unlock
  • License plate triggered gate/barrier access

Door Hardware & Control

  • Electric strikes and magnetic locks
  • Electronic door hardware (mortise, cylindrical)
  • REX (request-to-exit) sensor integration
  • Door position switch monitoring
  • Automatic door operator integration
  • Turnstile and gate controller integration

Management Platforms

  • Avigilon Alta (cloud) — multi-site, mobile-first
  • Genetec Security Center — enterprise on-prem
  • Verkada Access — unified cloud security
  • Role-based access levels and time schedules
  • Visitor management and temporary credentials
  • HR system integration for auto-provisioning
